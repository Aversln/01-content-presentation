Our services

VantaGrid Security is a future-focused cybersecurity studio built for a world of AI-generated attacks, autonomous malware, and always-on cloud systems. We help teams reduce risk with clear priorities, strong defaults, and evidence you can trust.

Threat modeling and risk mapping

We map your systems, data flows, and realistic attacker paths. You get a ranked risk plan tied to fixes your team can ship quickly, plus a short executive summary that explains what matters and why.

Cloud and API hardening

We harden cloud accounts, containers, CI/CD pipelines, and APIs. We focus on identity, secrets, segmentation, and logging so your platform is secure by default and easier to operate.

Pen testing and red-team sprints

We simulate real attacks against web apps, internal systems, and critical workflows. Findings come with clear reproduction steps, fix guidance, and a retest to confirm the issue is actually closed.

AI security and model protection

We test AI features for prompt injection, data leakage, unsafe tool use, and training-data risks. We then design guardrails and monitoring so your AI stays useful without becoming an open door.

Incident readiness and response

We build incident runbooks and response checklists that match your tools and team. If something happens, you contain faster, recover cleaner, and improve without guessing.

Security training for real teams

Practical training that fits how people work: phishing resistance, passwordless habits, safe sharing, and what to do when something feels off. Short sessions, real examples, measurable improvement.
