Services that ship security

We help teams reduce risk without slowing down delivery. Our work is practical: evidence-driven assessments, clean implementation guidance, and proof that the fixes actually hold.

How we work

We start by mapping what matters (systems, data, and business goals), then we test and harden the places attackers actually target. You get a prioritized plan, not a scary PDF.

What we offer

Threat modeling and risk mapping

We map your systems, data flows, and realistic attacker paths. You get a ranked risk list tied to fixes your team can ship quickly.

Cloud and API hardening

We lock down cloud accounts, containers, CI/CD pipelines, and APIs. Identity, secrets, segmentation, and logging are built with safe defaults that scale.

Pen testing and red-team sprints

We simulate real attacks against your apps and infrastructure. Findings include clear reproduction steps, fix guidance, and a retest to confirm it’s handled.

Incident readiness and response

We build runbooks and response checklists that match your tools and people. If an incident happens, you contain faster, recover cleaner, and learn without guessing.
